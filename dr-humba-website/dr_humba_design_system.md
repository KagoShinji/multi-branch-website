# Design Style & Brand Identity Guidelines: Dr. Humba

This document serves as a comprehensive visual and design reference for the **Dr. Humba** brand identity. AI systems, developers, and UI/UX designers should utilize these technical specs, color codes, typographic hierarchies, and stylistic principles to ensure absolute consistency across all assets, user interfaces, and marketing materials.

---

## 1. Brand Concept & Visual Identity
* **Core Theme:** Playful, approachable, and highly energetic fusion of culinary identity and a medical/clinical motif. 
* **Mascot Character:** A charismatic, winking pink pig with spiked hair, expressing joy and high enthusiasm.
* **Integrative Elements:** The character is wrapped in a light blue stethoscope. The chestpiece of the stethoscope doubles as a badge containing the letters **"DH"** (Dr. Humba) styled in a bubbly, cloud-like frame.
* **Overall Design Aesthetic:** Pop-art / Modern Cartoon Illustration. Clean vector line work with heavy, bold black outlines combined with vibrant, high-contrast flat fills and subtle gradient shading highlights.

---

## 2. Color Palette (Hex Codes & Usage)

The palette relies heavily on soft, friendly pinks anchored by striking black outlines, balanced with energetic cyan/blue and bright yellow accents.

### Primary Brand Colors
* **Mascot Body & Text Pink:** `#F7A3B2` (A rich, warm, semi-saturated bubblegum pink used for the large "DR." background text and the "HUMBA" wordmark).
* **Mascot Shadow Pink:** `#E08598` (Deepened pink shade used for vector depth, shadows under the ears, snout, and neck).
* **Mascot Base Skin:** `#F9B7C7` (Lighter bubblegum pink tone for the primary face and skin highlights).

### Accent & Diagnostic Colors
* **Stethoscope Tube Blue:** `#5ECBE6` (Vibrant sky/cyan blue providing a crisp, cooling contrast against the warm pink tones).
* **Stethoscope Highlight:** `#A3E7F5` (Very soft pastel cyan used for a glossy reflections on the stethoscope tubing and badge background).
* **Accent Yellow:** `#FDE047` / `#F8E247` (Bright lemon yellow used exclusively for the "DH" letters in the stethoscope chestpiece badge and internal ear accents).
* **Mouth Interior Deep Red:** `#9A1C32` (Deep crimson for the interior background of the open mouth).
* **Tongue Pink:** `#EF4444` / `#F43F5E` (Vibrant energetic red-pink highlighting the tongue).

### Contrast Anchors
* **Line Work / Contours:** `#1A1A1A` (Solid near-black ink style thick borders that define the cartoon style).
* **Pure Highlights:** `#FFFFFF` (Crisp white accents used for eye reflections, teeth, and glossy text depth).

---

## 3. Typography & Wordmark Specifications

### Font Characteristics
* **Style:** Ultra-bold, heavy-weight geometric sans-serif with heavily rounded corners (Soft-Display / Bubble Sans).
* **Casing:** All-Caps (`DR. HUMBA`).
* **Visual Weight:** Extremely dense, tightly tracked, minimizing negative space between characters to emphasize a bold, friendly structural block.
* **Arrangement:** * `DR.` operates as a massive structural background block framing the mascot.
  * `HUMBA` serves as the primary base wordmark anchoring the design at the bottom.

### Recommended System/Web Typography Alternatives
When replicating this brand layout in web applications, interfaces, or media generation, use the following typographic pairings:
* **Primary Headers / Headings (Logo Mimicry):** *Fredoka One*, *DynaPuff*, *Alka*, or *Baloo 2* (Extra Bold).
* **Body Text / UI Element Labels:** *Nunito* (Bold/Semi-Bold) or *Quicksand* for clean, highly legible rounded sans-serif continuity.

---

## 4. Structural Layout & Composition Geometry
* **Layering Order:**
  1. **Background Layer:** Gigantic `DR.` text elements acting as a graphic backdrop.
  2. **Middle Layer:** Character face centered slightly to the left, breaks out of the contours of the background text to create depth.
  3. **Foreground Layer:** Stethoscope tubing wrapping around the neck/chin, looping to the lower-left into a floating badge element.
  4. **Base Anchor Layer:** The text `HUMBA` aligned horizontally at the bottom with proportional spacing relative to the mascot asset above.
* **Negative Space Treatment:** The composition utilizes negative white space inside the loops of the text and stethoscope curves to maintain strong visibility and contrast even when scaled down to favicon or application icon sizes.

## 5. AI System Prompting Directives

When directing image generation models (e.g., Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) or UI design tools to scale this visual identity, utilize the following precise prompt fragments:

### For Asset Generation
> `Pop-art modern cartoon mascot logo of a winking pink pig with spiky hair, cheerful expression, wearing a vibrant cyan blue stethoscope around its neck, bubblegum pink and pastel blue color palette, thick clean black outlines, flat vector art, high contrast, smooth solid fills, minimal shading, white background --no realistic details, gradients, 3d render`

### For UI/UX Implementation
* Keep backgrounds clean and solid (preferably `#FFFFFF` or very faint cool greys like `#F8FAFC`).
* Use the primary brand pink (`#F7A3B2`) for primary interaction states, buttons, or active navigation nodes.
* Use the stethoscope blue (`#5ECBE6`) as a refreshing secondary accent color for notifications, success states, or subheadings to break up pink monochromatic fatigue.
